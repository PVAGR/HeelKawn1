## 📖 **FULL DESCRIPTION** (Main page content)

```html
<h1>HeelKawn - A Persistent Myth Engine</h1>

<p><strong>HeelKawn is a deterministic 2D world simulation</strong> where history is computed, not scripted. Memory does not decay randomly, history does not lie, and persistence is earned strictly by impact.</p>

<p>Think: <em>Dwarf Fortress</em> meets <em>RimWorld</em> meets <em>Crusader Kings</em> — with a focus on emergent storytelling and multi-generational legacy.</p>

<hr>

<h2>🎮 Key Features</h2>

<h3>📖 Text-Rich Storytelling</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Pawn Narratives</strong> - Every pawn has a readable life story with skills, family, and memories</li>
  <li><strong>Settlement Legends</strong> - Each settlement develops unique myths based on its actual history</li>
  <li><strong>Full Biographies</strong> - Click death notifications to read complete life stories</li>
  <li><strong>Chronicle View</strong> - Settlement history told as an organized story by year</li>
</ul>

<h3>🗿 Knowledge Preservation</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>18 Knowledge Types</strong> - Fire keeping, tool making, diplomacy, leadership, and more</li>
  <li><strong>Inscribed Stones</strong> - Carve knowledge on stone to preserve it forever</li>
  <li><strong>Interactive Reading</strong> - Right-click stones to read inscribed knowledge</li>
  <li><strong>Knowledge Death</strong> - If last carrier dies without inscribing, knowledge is lost forever</li>
</ul>

<h3>👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Dynasty System</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Family Trees</strong> - Track generations visually with clickable dynasty tree UI</li>
  <li><strong>Legacy Scoring</strong> - Measure your impact (children, knowledge, buildings, students)</li>
  <li><strong>Succession</strong> - Heirs inherit from ancestors when they die</li>
  <li><strong>Endgame Goals</strong> - Complete runs by achieving legacy milestones</li>
</ul>

<h3>🎭 Incarnation Mode</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Spectator Mode</strong> - Omniscient view with full UI (default)</li>
  <li><strong>Incarnated Mode</strong> - Experience world through pawn's senses (UI hides)</li>
  <li><strong>Knowledge Fog</strong> - Only know what your pawn knows when incarnated</li>
  <li><strong>Multiple Lives</strong> - Incarnate as different pawns across generations</li>
</ul>

<h3>⚔️ Emergent Social Dynamics</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Grudges</strong> - Pawns remember wrongs, inherit family feuds across generations</li>
  <li><strong>Gossip</strong> - Information spreads through social proximity</li>
  <li><strong>Reputation</strong> - Emerges from aggregated gossip, affects trust and cooperation</li>
  <li><strong>Avoidance AI</strong> - Pawns physically avoid enemies during pathfinding</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 How to Play</h2>

<h3>Basic Controls</h3>
<table>
  <tr><td><strong>Click pawn</strong></td><td>Select & view narrative</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>P</strong></td><td>Toggle incarnation mode</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>1-7</strong></td><td>Simulation speed (1x → 100x)</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Space</strong></td><td>Pause/Resume</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>F10</strong></td><td>Debug/creator menu (13+ features)</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Esc</strong></td><td>Deselect pawn</td></tr>
</table>

<h3>Getting Started</h3>
<ol>
  <li><strong>Launch game</strong> - World generates with 20 starter pawns</li>
  <li><strong>Watch notifications</strong> - Births, deaths, knowledge inscribed appear on right</li>
  <li><strong>Click pawns</strong> - See their narratives, skills, family</li>
  <li><strong>Speed up</strong> - Press 3 (26x) for faster simulation</li>
  <li><strong>Open F10</strong> - Explore all features (chronicles, legends, dynasty tree, etc.)</li>
</ol>

<h3>Endgame Goals</h3>
<p>Complete a "run" by achieving:</p>
<ul>
  <li>✅ <strong>1000 Legacy Score</strong> (from children, knowledge, buildings, students)</li>
  <li>✅ <strong>3 Dynasties Founded</strong></li>
  <li>✅ <strong>20 Dynasty Members</strong></li>
  <li>✅ <strong>3 Player Incarnations</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>Track progress in <strong>F10 → #75 Endgame Status</strong></p>
```
