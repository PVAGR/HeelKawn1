## 🏆 Success Metrics

**Good Multi-Track Collaboration:**
- ✅ Multiple AIs working simultaneously
- ✅ Cross-track references in session reports
- ✅ Integrated features (not siloed)
- ✅ Macro progress visible across all tracks
- ✅ AIs helping each other debug/design

**Bad (Avoid):**
- ❌ One AI at a time, waiting for others
- ❌ No references to other tracks' work
- ❌ Duplicate work (two AIs building same thing)
- ❌ Siloed development (no integration)
