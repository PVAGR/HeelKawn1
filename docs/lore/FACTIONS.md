# FACTIONS

Status: Canon faction summary
Scope: High-level identity seeds for future settlement and culture work

## Purpose

Factions in HeelKawn are emergent identities formed by deterministic history. They are not spawned as heroic archetypes; they arise from scars, recovery, architecture, and repeated social pressure.

## Current Seed Identities

### Open Hearth Clusters

- Origin region: low-scar recovery zones
- Identity driver: trust in stable resource patterns
- Behavioral pattern: expansion and rebuilding
- Architecture markers: broader shared spaces and accessible paths
- Current trajectory: likely to revive abandoned-but-quiet settlements

### Stoneward Enclaves

- Origin region: frequently bloodied regions
- Identity driver: memory of repeated loss
- Behavioral pattern: defense-first development
- Architecture markers: perimeter-heavy builds and constrained entries
- Current trajectory: high persistence, low outward integration

## Canon Rules

- Faction identity must come from recorded world history.
- Factions may influence architecture and settlement behavior, but they do not override the deterministic kernel.
- If faction behavior conflicts with current canon, update the canon source and note the change in the changelog.
